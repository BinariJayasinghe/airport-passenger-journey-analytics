# Airport Passenger Journey Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes airport passenger journeys to identify operational bottlenecks, improve passenger satisfaction, and support data-driven decision making.

The solution combines Business Analysis techniques with Python, SQL, and Power BI to transform passenger data into actionable business insights.

---

## Business Problem

Airports face increasing passenger volumes which create congestion and delays across important journey touchpoints including:

- Check-in
- Security Screening
- Immigration
- Boarding
- Baggage Claim

These delays reduce passenger satisfaction and negatively impact airport operational efficiency.

---

## Project Objectives

- Identify operational bottlenecks
- Monitor passenger satisfaction
- Analyze delay patterns
- Evaluate terminal utilization
- Support airport management decisions
- Improve passenger experience

## Key Business Insights

- Baggage Claim is the largest operational bottleneck.
- Moderate delays account for the majority of delay incidents.
- Passenger satisfaction remains relatively high despite delays.
- Peak traffic periods contribute significantly to congestion.
- Domestic and international passenger volumes remain balanced.

---

## Recommendations

1. Increase staffing during peak periods.
2. Optimize baggage handling operations.
3. Improve check-in automation.
4. Introduce predictive delay monitoring.
5. Enhance passenger communication systems.
