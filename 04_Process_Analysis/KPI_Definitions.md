# KPI Definitions

## Operational KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| Average Check-in Time | Average time spent in check-in queues | < 10 minutes |
| Average Security Wait Time | Average waiting time at security screening | < 8 minutes |
| Average Immigration Processing Time | Average immigration processing duration | < 12 minutes |
| Average Boarding Time | Average boarding duration per flight | < 15 minutes |
| Average Baggage Claim Time | Average time taken for baggage delivery | < 20 minutes |

---

## Customer Experience KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| Passenger Satisfaction Score | Average customer satisfaction rating | > 4.5/5 |
| Complaint Rate | Number of complaints per 1,000 passengers | < 5 |
| Missed Flight Rate | Percentage of passengers missing flights due to delays | < 1% |

---

## Efficiency KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|-----------|--------|
| Queue Utilization Rate | Percentage utilization of service counters | 70%-85% |
| Staff Utilization Rate | Percentage utilization of operational staff | 75%-90% |
| On-Time Boarding Rate | Percentage of flights boarded on time | > 95% |

---

## Dashboard KPIs

The Power BI dashboard will monitor:

- Passenger volume
- Waiting times
- Satisfaction scores
- Peak-hour congestion
- Staff utilization
- Operational bottlenecks
