# Bottleneck Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this analysis is to identify operational bottlenecks that negatively impact passenger experience and airport efficiency.

---

## Identified Bottlenecks

| Process Stage | Bottleneck | Business Impact | Severity |
|--------------|-----------|----------------|----------|
| Check-in | Long queues during peak hours | Passenger dissatisfaction and missed flights | High |
| Security Screening | Limited screening lanes and manual inspections | Increased waiting times | High |
| Immigration | Slow document verification process | Processing delays | High |
| Boarding | Congestion near boarding gates | Boarding delays | Medium |
| Baggage Claim | Delayed baggage delivery | Poor passenger experience | Medium |

---

## Root Causes

### Check-in
- Insufficient counters during peak periods.
- Uneven passenger distribution across terminals.

### Security Screening
- Limited staffing during busy hours.
- Manual bag inspections increase processing time.

### Immigration
- Staff shortages.
- High volume of international travelers.

### Boarding
- Passengers arriving at gates simultaneously.
- Inefficient boarding group allocation.

### Baggage Claim
- Delays in baggage unloading.
- Poor carousel assignment planning.

---

## Recommended Actions

| Bottleneck | Recommendation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Check-in | Introduce self-service kiosks and dynamic counter allocation |
| Security | Use predictive analytics for staffing allocation |
| Immigration | Optimize staffing schedules using demand forecasts |
| Boarding | Introduce staggered boarding groups |
| Baggage Claim | Improve baggage routing and carousel assignment |

---

## Priority Ranking

1. Security Screening
2. Check-in
3. Immigration
4. Boarding
