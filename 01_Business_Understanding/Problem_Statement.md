# Problem Statement

## Current Situation

SkyConnect International Airport is experiencing increased passenger volumes, resulting in congestion and delays across multiple stages of the passenger journey.

Passengers frequently experience long waiting times during:

- Check-in
- Security screening
- Immigration
- Boarding
- Baggage claim

These delays negatively impact customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

---

## Business Problem

Airport management currently lacks a centralized analytical solution capable of monitoring passenger movement and identifying operational bottlenecks across the passenger journey.

As a result:

- Passenger satisfaction is decreasing.
- Resource allocation decisions are reactive rather than proactive.
- Operational inefficiencies are difficult to identify and address.
- Management lacks visibility into passenger flow and service performance.

---

## Impact

The current situation may lead to:

- Increased operational costs
- Reduced passenger loyalty
- Negative airport reputation
- Reduced competitiveness among regional airports

---

## Desired Future State

The airport requires a data-driven analytics platform capable of:

- Monitoring passenger flow in real time.
- Identifying bottlenecks across airport operations.
- Supporting proactive decision-making.
- Improving passenger experience and operational efficiency.
