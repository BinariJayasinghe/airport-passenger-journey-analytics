# Airport Passenger Journey Analytics

## Business Case

**Project Name:** Airport Passenger Journey Analytics

**Organization:** SkyConnect International Airport (Fictional)

**Prepared By:** Binari Jayasinghe

**Role:** Business Analyst

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Draft
---

## 1. Executive Summary

SkyConnect International Airport is experiencing increasing passenger waiting times across key journey touchpoints such as check-in, security screening, immigration, boarding, and baggage claim. These delays negatively affect passenger satisfaction, airport efficiency, and overall service quality.

This project proposes an Airport Passenger Journey Analytics solution to identify operational bottlenecks, monitor key performance indicators, and support data-driven decision-making. The solution will combine Business Analysis documentation, data preparation, SQL analysis, and Power BI dashboards to improve passenger experience and operational performance.
---

## 2. Background

SkyConnect International Airport serves millions of passengers annually and manages operations across multiple journey stages including check-in, security screening, immigration, boarding, and baggage claim.

As passenger volumes continue to increase, airport operations have become more complex. Delays and congestion at individual touchpoints can create ripple effects throughout the passenger journey, reducing operational efficiency and negatively affecting customer satisfaction.

Currently, operational data exists across multiple systems and departments, making it difficult for management to obtain a complete view of passenger flow and service performance.
---

## 3. Business Problem

SkyConnect International Airport is experiencing increasing passenger dissatisfaction due to long waiting times, operational bottlenecks, and inconsistent service performance across various stages of the passenger journey.

Airport management currently lacks a centralized analytical solution capable of monitoring passenger flow, identifying congestion points, and measuring operational performance across departments.

As a result:

- Passenger satisfaction scores are declining.
- Airport staff allocation is inefficient during peak hours.
- Operational bottlenecks are identified reactively rather than proactively.
- Management decisions are based on fragmented data sources rather than comprehensive insights.

Without a data-driven approach, the airport risks reduced customer loyalty, increased operational costs, and damage to its reputation.
---

## 4. Business Opportunity

The challenges currently faced by SkyConnect International Airport present an opportunity to transform airport operations through data-driven decision-making.

By implementing an Airport Passenger Journey Analytics solution, the airport can:

- Identify operational bottlenecks across the passenger journey.
- Improve resource allocation during peak periods.
- Reduce passenger waiting times.
- Increase passenger satisfaction and loyalty.
- Improve airport operational efficiency.
- Enable proactive decision-making through real-time insights.
- Support future passenger growth without compromising service quality.

This initiative aligns with the airport's digital transformation strategy and commitment to delivering an exceptional passenger experience.
---

## 5. Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is the development of an Airport Passenger Journey Analytics platform that provides end-to-end visibility into passenger movement and operational performance across the airport.

The solution will consist of:

- Business Analysis documentation to define business needs and requirements.
- A centralized passenger journey dataset covering key airport touchpoints.
- Python-based data preparation and feature engineering.
- SQL analysis for operational insights and KPI measurement.
- Interactive Power BI dashboards for executives and operational managers.
- Business recommendations based on analytical findings.

The platform will enable airport management to monitor performance indicators, identify bottlenecks, optimize resource allocation, and improve passenger satisfaction through data-driven decision-making.
---

## 6. Business Objectives

The Airport Passenger Journey Analytics project aims to achieve the following objectives:

1. Reduce average passenger waiting time by 20% within 12 months.
2. Increase passenger satisfaction scores from 3.8/5 to 4.5/5.
3. Improve operational efficiency through better resource allocation.
4. Reduce boarding and baggage-related delays.
5. Enable airport management to make data-driven decisions using real-time insights.
6. Support future passenger growth without negatively impacting service quality.

## 9. Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Poor data quality | High | Implement data validation and cleansing procedures |
| Missing operational data | Medium | Use assumptions and document data limitations |
| Resistance to change | Medium | Engage stakeholders early and provide training |

## 10. Assumptions

- Passenger operational data is available and accessible.
- Airport departments are willing to share relevant data.
- Historical data accurately represents operational performance.
- Stakeholders support the implementation of analytics-driven improvements.
---

## 11. Constraints

- Budget limitations for new technology investments.
- Data privacy and security regulations.
- Limited availability of historical operational data.
- Project timeline and resource constraints.

## 12. Recommendation

It is recommended that SkyConnect International Airport proceed with the Airport Passenger Journey Analytics initiative to improve passenger experience and operational efficiency.

The project provides a structured approach to identifying bottlenecks, improving resource allocation, and enabling data-driven decision-making through analytics and business intelligence technologies.

The expected operational and customer benefits significantly outweigh the implementation effort, making this initiative a valuable investment in the airport's digital transformation journey.
