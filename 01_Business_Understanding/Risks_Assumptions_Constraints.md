# Risks, Assumptions, and Constraints

## Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|--------|--------------------|
| Poor data quality | High | Perform data validation and cleansing |
| Missing operational data | Medium | Define assumptions and document limitations |
| Resistance to process changes | Medium | Engage stakeholders early |
| Delays in dashboard development | Low | Use phased delivery and milestone tracking |

---

## Assumptions

- Historical passenger data is available and reliable.
- Airport departments are willing to share operational data.
- Passenger behavior patterns remain relatively stable during the analysis period.
- Stakeholders support the implementation of analytics-driven improvements.

---

## Constraints

- Limited access to real airport operational systems.
- Project uses simulated or publicly available datasets.
- Budget and resource limitations for implementation.
- Compliance with data privacy and security regulations.
