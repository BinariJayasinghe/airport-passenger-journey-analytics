# SMART Objectives

## Objective 1
Reduce average passenger waiting time across airport touchpoints by **20% within 12 months** of implementing the analytics solution.

- Specific: Reduce waiting times.
- Measurable: 20% reduction.
- Achievable: Through operational optimization and analytics.
- Relevant: Improves passenger experience.
- Time-bound: Within 12 months.

---

## Objective 2
Increase passenger satisfaction scores from **3.8/5 to 4.5/5 within one year**.

- Specific: Improve satisfaction scores.
- Measurable: Increase to 4.5/5.
- Achievable: By reducing delays and improving service.
- Relevant: Supports customer experience goals.
- Time-bound: Within one year.

---

## Objective 3
Reduce average baggage claim time to **under 15 minutes within 9 months**.

- Specific: Improve baggage handling efficiency.
- Measurable: Under 15 minutes.
- Achievable: Through bottleneck analysis.
- Relevant: Improves passenger experience.
- Time-bound: Within 9 months.

---

## Objective 4
Provide airport management with **real-time KPI dashboards within 6 months** of project initiation.

- Specific: Deliver dashboards.
- Measurable: Dashboard availability.
- Achievable: Through Power BI implementation.
- Relevant: Supports decision-making.
- Time-bound: Within 6 months.
