---

## Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to improve airport operational efficiency and passenger satisfaction by identifying bottlenecks across the passenger journey and enabling data-driven decision-making through analytics and business intelligence solutions.

---

## Project Objectives

- Reduce passenger waiting times.
- Improve passenger satisfaction scores.
- Optimize staff and resource allocation.
- Improve operational visibility.
- Support proactive decision-making.

---

## Project Scope

### In Scope
- Check-in process
- Security screening
- Immigration process
- Boarding process
- Baggage claim process
- Passenger satisfaction analysis
- Power BI dashboard development

### Out of Scope
- Airline scheduling optimization
- Aircraft maintenance operations
- Airport retail operations
- Flight route planning

---

## Key Stakeholders

- Airport Executive Management
- Operations Managers
- Security Department
- Ground Operations Team
- Customer Experience Team
- Passengers

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- Average passenger waiting times decrease.
- Passenger satisfaction improves.
- Operational bottlenecks are identified earlier.
- Management can monitor KPIs using dashboards.

## Estimated Deliverables

- Business Analysis documentation
- Stakeholder analysis
- Requirements documentation
- Process analysis artifacts
- Cleaned dataset
- SQL analysis
- Power BI dashboard
- Business recommendations
