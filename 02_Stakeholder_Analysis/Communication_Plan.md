# Communication Plan

| Stakeholder | Information Required | Communication Method | Frequency | Owner |
|------------|---------------------|----------------------|-----------|-------|
| Airport CEO | Project progress, KPIs, risks | Executive report and meetings | Monthly | Business Analyst |
| Operations Manager | Operational insights, issues, milestones | Meetings and reports | Weekly | Business Analyst |
| IT Department | Technical requirements and implementation updates | Technical workshops | Weekly | Business Analyst |
| Security Department | Queue and security process analysis | Workshops and emails | Bi-weekly | Business Analyst |
| Customer Experience Team | Passenger satisfaction insights | Reports and presentations | Monthly | Business Analyst |
| Ground Operations Team | Boarding and baggage insights | Meetings | Bi-weekly | Business Analyst |

---

## Communication Objectives

- Ensure stakeholder alignment throughout the project lifecycle.
- Communicate project progress transparently.
- Identify and resolve issues early.
- Facilitate collaboration between departments.
- Support informed decision-making.

---

## Communication Channels

- Meetings
- Email updates
- Progress reports
- Workshops
- Dashboard demonstrations
- Executive presentations
